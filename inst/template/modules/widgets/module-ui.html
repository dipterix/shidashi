Flip box

Flip box provides a box that has two sides. There are three ways to trigger the flip box:

  • active_on="click": the boxes flip when clicking both the front side and the back side.
  • active_on="click-front": the boxes flip only when clicking the front side and you have to manually flip them back by calling flip("widget id") in R or window.shidashi.flipBox("widget id") in JavaScript.
  • active_on="manual": the boxes flip manually. You have to call flip("widget id") in R or window.shidashi.flipBox("widget id") in JavaScript.
